Best Movies According to Sourav

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Blade Runner 2049 (2017)

Director Denis Villeneuve continues the dystopian journey with stunning visuals and a thought-provoking narrative that explores the nature of humanity and artificial intelligence.

Ex Machina (2014)

Alex Garland's directorial debut is a tense and cerebral thriller that delves into the ethical implications of creating sentient AI, with exceptional performances from the cast.

Arrival (2016)

A mesmerizing and intellectually stimulating film directed by Denis Villeneuve that combines language, time, and the concept of first contact with aliens.
